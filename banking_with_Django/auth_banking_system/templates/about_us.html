About XYZ Bank

Welcome to XYZ Bank, a modern online banking platform designed to make financial services simple, secure, and accessible to everyone. Our goal is to bring banking to your fingertips – safe, fast, and reliable.

🌟 Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver secure and innovative banking solutions that empower individuals and businesses to manage their finances effectively, anytime and anywhere.

👁️ Our Vision

To become the most trusted digital banking platform by combining cutting-edge technology with user-friendly services that meet the needs of today’s generation.

💻 Technologies We Use

  • Backend: Django (Python framework)
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
  • Database: SQLite / PostgreSQL
  • Security: Django authentication, password hashing, session management

⚡ Why Choose Our App?

  • ✔️ Easy and secure login system with authentication
  • ✔️ Simple account management (check balance, profile, details)
  • ✔️ Quick and safe transactions
  • ✔️ Online loan application
  • ✔️ Responsive design – works on mobile and desktop

📌 Note (For Project Purpose)

This project is developed as part of a Banking Web Application for learning and demonstration purposes. It showcases how banking features like authentication, transactions, and account management can be built using Django.
